AppKeelaa

AppKeelaa is the application that offers a variety of services for athletes or other people who are interested in sport. We are the channel to help you enjoy sports issues more conveniently. We cover all key features within our application including:

Registration

We combine all sport events that will be held at that time. We also provide registration platform for our application users who want to participate in the sport events. - Payment meth

Payment method

We help our users summarize their payment lists and provide the payment method for our users that will be occurred after registration step.

Accommodation and transportation choices

For people who are far away from the location of the sport events, we provide the choices of accommodation and transportation to be convenient for our users.

Statistic of all participants

When you participant in each sport event, we collect all of your data as statistic in your portfolio.

Sports equipment distribution

We provide sports equipment distribution channel for them in this application because sport equipment is important for people especially athletes to play the sport and prevent the injuries.

Photo platform

When you participate at the sport event or play sport, we can help you create good memory by providing photo platform. This platform helps you take your photo when playing sport at the event. If you want to get that platform, you have to pay a little more for this service.

Sports news notification

Our service provides updated sports news, so all our users can get news on time and they can share the news to discuss with other group of people who are interested in the same field.

Point collection for discount or redeem

When you participate in each sports event, you can get the point in your portfolio. We offer many lists of compensation reward for our users such as discount and redeem that you can get by using your point collection depending on your purpose.
